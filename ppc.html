Palmetto Power Clean

A friend of mine is starting a power washing business, and he wanted a website to market his services. To begin thinking about building his website, I decided to redesign the website of a local power washing company, Palmetto Power Clean.

Palmetto Power Clean's current site lacks visual hierarchy. While there is a lot of great content, it is jumbled and can be repetitive. The payment link is located in a list of service awards, creating confusion. In the general services, commercial services and before & after section the content overlaps quite a bit. The site is not responsive.

Current Site

    Goals

  • point users to two main calls to action: pay bill and request an estimate
  • responsive layout
  • update look of site, give it a professional tone
  • create visual hierarchy and organize content, reduce clutter
  • highlight social media, before and after photos, awards and customer reviews
